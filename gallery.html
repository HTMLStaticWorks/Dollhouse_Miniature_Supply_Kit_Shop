Crafting Guide

Understanding Miniature Scales

Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned builder, understanding our standard scale ratios makes building your dream world easy.

Popular

1:12 Classic Scale

1" MIN. = 12" REAL

The absolute gold standard for collectors. Allows for high-fidelity detailing, real brass electrical wiring, working LED fixtures, and intricate luxury furniture sets.

Intermediate

1:24 Cozy Scale

1" MIN. = 24" REAL

A gorgeous and space-saving alternative. Ideal for desktop room boxes, shelf displays, and charming handcrafted scenes that fit easily in any living space.

Micro-Scale

1:48 Micro Scale

1" MIN. = 48" REAL

The pinnacle of ultra-miniature craftsmanship. Used for glass dome terrariums, tiny tea-tin dioramas, and micro-gardens where every millimeter counts.

Specialty

1:144 Micro-Micro Scale

1" MIN. = 144" REAL

The whimsical 'dollhouse inside a dollhouse' scale. These microscopic builds are tiny enough to sit on a mantlepiece or bookcase shelf inside your 1:12 scale dollhouse room.
