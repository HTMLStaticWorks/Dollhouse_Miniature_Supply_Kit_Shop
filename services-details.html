Frequently Asked Questions

Do the kits include glue?
Yes, we include a precision applicator of professional-grade wood glue in every kit.
Can I paint the wood?
Absolutely! The wood is pre-sanded and ready for your choice of stain or acrylic paint.
What scale are the kits?
All our furniture kits are standard 1:12 scale unless otherwise noted.
