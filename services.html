Service Questions

What is the typical lead time for custom commissions?
Most custom projects take between 4 to 12 weeks depending on the complexity and scale of the request.
Do you ship built dollhouses or just kits?
We ship both! Kits are sent worldwide, while fully built dollhouses are shipped via specialized furniture couriers to ensure safety.
Are your lighting systems compatible with standard batteries?
Yes, most of our systems use standard button cell batteries, but we also offer USB and AC adapter options for larger displays.
