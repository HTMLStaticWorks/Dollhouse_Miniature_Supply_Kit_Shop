Bespoke Commissions

Have a specific vision for a tiny world? Our master artists take on a limited number of museum-grade custom commissions each year. From replicating family ancestral homes in perfect 1:12 scale to crafting high-fidelity historical rooms or fantastical cinematic scale sets, we bring your architectural dreams to life.

Our Bespoke Process:

  • 1
    Detailed Design Consultation

    Share blueprints, real-world photos, or concept art. We'll consult with you to map out exact scales, materials, and internal lighting systems.

  • 2
    Fabrication & Curation

    Our woodcarvers, textile specialists, and electronics engineers construct your model using luxury hardwoods, fine fabrics, and microscopic 12V LED circuits.

  • 3
    Quality Inspection & Safe Delivery

    Upon completion, we perform high-detail photography checks and package your masterpiece in shock-absorbent custom crating for global transit.

Popular Commission Examples:

Recent successful commissions include replicating a 19th-century French Bakery, building an immersive Gothic Cathedral Room Box, and engineering a working Steampunk Laboratory complete with custom copper piping and moving micro-wheels.

Service Questions

What is the typical lead time for custom commissions?
Most custom projects take between 4 to 12 weeks depending on the complexity and scale of the request.
Do you ship built dollhouses or just kits?
We ship both! Kits are sent worldwide, while fully built dollhouses are shipped via specialized furniture couriers to ensure safety.
Are your lighting systems compatible with standard batteries?
Yes, most of our systems use standard button cell batteries, but we also offer USB and AC adapter options for larger displays.
