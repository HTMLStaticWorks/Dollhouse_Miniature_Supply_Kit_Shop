The Heart of Miniature Art

We believe that big dreams can fit in the palm of your hand. Our mission is to provide the finest materials for miniature artists worldwide.

Our Journey

2015

Founded in a small home garage with a single laser cutter and a passion for 1:12 scale furniture.

2017

Launched our first line of DIY room box kits, winning the "Creative Hobby of the Year" award.

2020

Expanded to international shipping, connecting with over 50,000 makers across the globe.

2026

Opening our new flagship workshop and artist residency program in Craft City.

Decorative Elements

Our Tiny Crafting World

Explore a handful of the meticulous 1:12 scale dollhouse elements and visual details that bring our worlds to life.

Intricate Tiny Furniture

Hand-finished mahogany desks, velvet armchairs, and styled kitchen cabinets with actual working drawers.

Working LED Lighting

Micro-engineered chandeliers, brass sconces, and candle setups wired with tiny warm-glowing 12V LED bulbs.

Micro Decors & Ceramics

Hand-painted ceramic plates, real glass goblets, and teeny copper pots that dress up scale rooms in rich detail.

Micro Garden Botanicals

Ultra-realistic moss, styled tiny potted boxwood hedges, and intricate paper-cut greenhouse florals.

How We Create

01

Design

Meticulous 3D modeling of historical and modern architectural pieces, ensuring every millimeter is historically accurate.

02

Prototyping

Testing every joint and fit to ensure a seamless build experience for our makers, no matter their skill level.

03

Production

Precision laser cutting and hand-finishing each individual part in our artisan workshop using sustainably sourced woods.

04

Curation

Pairing each kit with the perfect luxury fabrics, specialized paints, and precision lighting systems for a museum-finish.
